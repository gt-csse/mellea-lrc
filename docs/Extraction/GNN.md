# Overview

## Description

### Hypothesis

Can we identify or determine if a case citation is used incorrectly in a piece of legal text by analyzing its network (i.e., to whom are they connected)? 

### Theories

- A case citations network follows the power law (i.e., a few cases are the most cited).
- Cases with similar neighbors are related.
